Frequently Asked Questions

How often can pistols and rim-fire rifles be shot?

PISTOLS MAY BE SHOT SEVEN DAYS PER WEEK
ALONG WITH RIM-FIRE RIFLES.

What are the allowed calibers?

5.56, 223, .300 Blackout, 30 Carbine, 30-30, 450 Bushmaster,
458 SOCOM, .50 Beowulf
NO Magnum Center fire rifles & NO AR-Pistols or AK-Pistols in center fire rifle calibers.

How often can center-fire rifles be shot?

CENTER-FIRE RIFLES UP TO 30-30 MAY ONLY BE SHOT ON WEDNESDAYS AND SUNDAYS, WITH THE EXCEPTION OF RIFLES CHAMBERED IN HANDGUN CALIBERS (I.e. 9MM CARBINES, 44 MAG, ETC).

Can you shoot suppressed rifles?

SUPPRESSED RIFLES MAY ALSO BE SHOT SEVEN DAYS PER WEEK.

How long is your range?

OUR RANGE IS 25 YARDS LONG.
